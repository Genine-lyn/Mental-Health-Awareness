Do's and Dont's to Improve your Mental Well-Being

do's and dont's to improve your mental well-being

Mental Health is a broad term that includes anything to do with our mind - the seat of behavior, emotions, and thinking. Mental well-being is simply when all the previously mentioned characteristics seated in the mind are working in tandem, or in harmony. It is also sometimes known as "mental hygiene". When we behave well, when we think positively, and when we experience and express good and positive emotions, then our mental well-being is being achieved. This also leads to having a fuller and more satisfying physical health. The following are some do's and dont's that will help you improve your mental well-being. This list is not exhaustive, but by practicing even some of the tips, your life will become happier and satisfying.

THINGS YOU SHOULD DO TO IMPROVE YOUR MENTAL WELL-BEING

1. ORGANIZE YOUR LIFE

Start by organizing every single day. Remove clutter from your home, cupboards, tables, work space, and more importantly - your mind. You will find an amazing change within few days.

2. IDENTIFY YOUR STRENGTHS AS WELL AS WEAKNESSES

Knowing what you can excel at enables you to aim higher and achieve much more. Knowing your weaknesses gives you a clearer understanding of things that may be holding you back, and you can then work around finding ways to not let your weaknesses pull you behind.

3. ACCEPT AND VALUE YOURSELF FOR WHAT YOU ARE

Determine and live your personal values.

4. EAT WELL, SLEEP WELL, EXERCISE, AND LOOK AFTER YOUR PHYSICAL HEALTH

Being physically active can help you lead a healthier and happier life.

5. DEVELOP SOME INTERESTS OR HOBBIES IN LIFE

Research shows that people who regularly engage in hobbies are less likely to suffer from stress and depression.

6. TAKE TIME OUT FOR MEDITATION AND PRAYER REGULARLY

Meditating and praying helps you focus on your inner self.

7. PRACTICE GRATITUDE

Expressing gratitude improves overall psychological health, by reducing the negative emotions that may be influencing our happiness and well-being.

THINGS YOU SHOULD NOT DO TO IMPROVE YOUR MENTAL WELL-BEING

1. DON'T REMAIN IDLE

Spend your free time with your family and friends. This will give you joy and help you keep mentally stable.

2. DON'T BECOME COMPLACEMENT

Take risks, think out of the box.

3. DON'T FISH FOR PRAISE OR COMPLIMETNS ALL THE TIME

Your happiness should not depend on hollow words of praise.

4. DON'S HATE ANYBODY

This is the darkest of emotions, and the deadliest.

5. DON'T COMPARE YOURSELF WITH OTHERS

Remember, in the whole wide world there is only one YOU.

6. DON'T GET ANGRY OR UPSET EASILY

Anger consumes huge amounts of mental energy, and clouds your thinking, making it harder to concentrate or enjoy life. It can also lead to stress, depression, and other mental health problems.

7. DON'T BLAME YOURSELF UNNECESSARILY

Be kind to yourself. Be realistic about your shortcomings, but also about your strength as well.
